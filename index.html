ABOUT US!

We are Formula Manipal, a team of 45 passionate engineers from Manipal, Karnataka, dedicated to designing, building, and racing Formula-style cars. Since 2007, we've built 14 cutting-edge vehicles, competing and winning at Formula Bharat and international events in Germany, Italy, Austria, the UK, and beyond. Driven by our motto, "Engineered with Passion," we push boundaries every year to create faster, more efficient, and more powerful cars. As we celebrate 17 years of innovation, stay tuned to watch us FLY!
